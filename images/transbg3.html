Global Field & Farm Seeds

Established in 2003, Global Field & Farm Seeds., specialize in breeding, production, processing anddistribution of vegetables seeds both open pollinated & hybrids with a focus on providing paramount quality seeds. The company caters to the requirement of delivering all climate seeds for farmers across the continent. At present, the company is engaged in production, marketing and import and export of vegetable seeds.

Global F&F Seeds is a family owned Seed Company with an extensive international presence,years of technical experience and a wide range of high quality vegetable seeds.
